Ready For Your Next Meal

Restaurants: Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine. Grocery Stores: Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Local Fish and Rice

A visit to Taniti wouldn’t be complete without trying the island’s staple dish: fresh local fish served with seasoned rice. This simple yet flavorful meal is a favorite among both locals and tourists, showcasing the island’s rich fishing traditions and fresh ingredients. Caught daily by Tanitian fishermen, the fish is often grilled, steamed, or pan-seared and paired with aromatic rice infused with local herbs and spices. Found in many of Taniti’s restaurants—from casual eateries to seaside shacks—this dish offers a true taste of the island’s culture and coastal charm.

American-style Meals

For visitors craving the familiar flavors of home, Taniti offers several restaurants that serve delicious American-style meals. From classic burgers and fries to hearty breakfasts, grilled sandwiches, and fresh salads, these spots provide comforting favorites in a tropical island setting. Perfect for families with picky eaters or anyone looking for a taste of the mainland, these restaurants combine familiar dishes with island hospitality. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or sitting down for a casual dinner, Taniti’s American-style options ensure there’s something for everyone.

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Taniti’s vibrant culinary scene includes flavorful Pan-Asian cuisine that reflects the island’s cultural diversity and love for fresh ingredients. Visitors can enjoy a variety of dishes inspired by Thai, Japanese, Chinese, and Vietnamese cooking—ranging from savory noodle bowls and sushi to stir-fried vegetables, dumplings, and rich curries. These restaurants blend traditional recipes with island-sourced ingredients, offering a unique dining experience that’s both authentic and tropical. Whether you’re in the mood for something spicy, savory, or sweet, Taniti’s Pan-Asian cuisine is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Supermarkets

Taniti’s supermarkets provide visitors with everything they need for a comfortable and convenient stay. The island has two full-sized supermarkets that offer a wide selection of groceries, household items, snacks, and locally sourced produce. These stores are perfect for travelers staying in vacation rentals or anyone looking to stock up on essentials during their trip. In addition to familiar international brands, you’ll also find unique local products that give a taste of Tanitian culture. With friendly service and easy accessibility, the supermarkets make self-catering on the island simple and stress-free.

Grocery Stores

In addition to its full-sized supermarkets, Taniti is home to several smaller grocery stores that offer a convenient and personal shopping experience. These neighborhood shops carry everyday essentials like snacks, beverages, toiletries, and fresh produce—perfect for quick stops or last-minute needs. Often located near popular lodging and residential areas, these stores are ideal for travelers who prefer to stay stocked without making a big trip. With friendly local owners and a laid-back atmosphere, shopping at Taniti’s smaller grocery stores is a great way to support the community while enjoying island life.

Convenience Store

For quick and easy access to everyday essentials, Taniti’s 24-hour convenience store is a reliable stop for both locals and tourists. Whether you need snacks, drinks, sunscreen, toiletries, or travel necessities, this store has you covered any time of day or night. Its central location makes it especially useful for late-night arrivals or early-morning departures. The convenience store is also a great option for grabbing refreshments before heading to the beach or exploring the island. Open around the clock, it ensures you’re never far from what you need during your stay in Taniti.
